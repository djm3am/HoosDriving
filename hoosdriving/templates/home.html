Welcome to Hoo's Driving

Hoo's Driving provides a safe and secure site to connect UVa students who want to find rides or passengers. It's ride-sharing for hoos and by hoos.

  • Find rides to nearby schools, home, or anywhere your fellow hoos are driving!
  • Securely transfer money for gas or fare
  • Feel safe knowing that your driver/passenger is a verified UVa student
