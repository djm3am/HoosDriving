More about us

Hoo's Driving is a service that connects UVa students looking to save money and be environmentally conscious by carpooling to local destinations. Our goal is to provide a safe and secure platform that takes the guesswork out of finding that ride to JMU or VCU for the weekend. Hoo's Driving helps you find a ride, easily schedule pickup and dropoff times, and securely pay for gas (or roadtrip snacks)

So nest time you're tired of hanging on Grounds and want to get away for a weekend or visit your family back home, let us take the headache out of finding a ride home! You can feel safe knowing Hoo's Driving You.
